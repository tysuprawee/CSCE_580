Easy Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies
By agreene2028 in Cooking

Supplies
Stand mixer
Medium bowl
Parchment paper
Soft spatula
Baking sheet

Ingredients
Flour
Baking soda
Baking powder
Salt
Coconut oil
Light brown sugar
Granulated sugar
Large egg
Vanilla extract
Old fashioned oats
Dark chocolate chips

Step 1: Get Out Your Ingredients/Preheat the Oven
For this step you will want to take out all of the ingredients above and preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step 2: Measure Out Dry Ingredients and Mix!
Measure out 1 cup of flour, 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Mix.

Step 3: Measure Out Wet Ingredients and Mix!
Measure out 1/2 cup of brown sugar, 1/4 cup granulated sugar, and 1/2 cup coconut oil. Mix.

Step 4: Add in the Egg and Vanilla
Once the wet ingredients are mixed together add in the egg and vanilla and mix until combined.

Step 5: Combining Wet and Dry
Once you have the mixed wet ingredients, slowly mix in the dry ingredients with the mixer on low.

Step 6: Add in the Oats and Chips
Once the wet ingredients and the dry ingredients are combined add in 2/3 cups of oats and 1 cup of chocolate chips and stir with spatula until combined.

Step 7: Form Dough Into Balls
Line your baking sheet with parchment paper and form dough into balls the size of 2 tablespoons, making 3 rows of 4. (Makes 2 batches for a total of 24 cookies)

Step 8: Bake!
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
